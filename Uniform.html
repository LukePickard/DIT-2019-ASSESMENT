Welcome to The WHS Uniform Page, We have a Mandatory Uniform That is available for purchase at our Uniform shop in Student Services. We feature a Shirt, Tie, Sweater, Blazer and students choice of pants or shorts. The girls wear a Kilt that varies between seasons. Our Uniform Represents our school, students proudly wear it as a symbol of pride. Not to mention Badges of certain acheivement such as "Level 2 Exellence" can be displayed on the students blazer alongside their House badge and extra curricular acheivements. Come speak to the lovely staff at our Student Services for more information. (Main entrance stairs on the left)
