Here at WHS we abide by a strict yet fair code of behaviour presented by the Board of Trustee's policy. We deeply value good behaviour and beleive in consequences to those who choose not to present this. Our School follows the prodecures recomended by the Board of Trustee's and multiple incidents require escalations, these will be dealt with by our Senior Leadership Team. WHS has strict laws against students abusing illicit substances including , Alchohol, Tobacco products including E-Cigarettes. Students who break these rules will follow our escalation procedure and a correct punishment will be determined. We include a student parking area for eligbile students to drive to school, we expect that all students respect this privilege and do not abuse this treatment.
